Why this dinner

Privacy policy moves forward when the right people can speak candidly.

CPD creates a smaller, conversational setting for direct engagement on financial privacy, privacy-preserving cryptography, and the enduring relevance of cypherpunk values in public policy.

The evening is designed to keep the conversation serious, policy-relevant, and grounded in cypherpunk values while welcoming aligned voices whose work can strengthen the case for privacy as a public good.

01

Financial privacy is normal

Everyday financial dignity deserves a serious place in the policy conversation.

02

Cryptography is essential

Privacy-preserving technology can support both individual rights and responsible systems.

03

Cypherpunk values endure

Open systems, user agency, and technical credibility remain relevant to public policy.
